Check-in & Check-out

Check-in is from 2 PM, and check-out is by 10 AM. Early check-in or late check-out may be possible depending on availability.

Location & Access

The beach is just a short walk away — about 180 meters (2–3 minutes).

You can access the Umhlanga Promenade directly from the beachfront path.

Yes. We are within walking distance of Umhlanga Village, which has a wide choice of restaurants, cafés, and shops — about a 5–7 minute walk.

King Shaka International Airport is around 15 minutes by car (≈ 18 km).

Apartment Details

The unit comfortably accommodates up to 6 guests in 3 bedrooms (2 bathrooms).

It’s a fully private self-catering unit with exclusive use of all amenities — balcony, kitchen, living area, and BBQ.

Yes! The balcony offers ocean views — perfect for morning coffee or sunset BBQs.

Yes, the building has an elevator for easy access.
